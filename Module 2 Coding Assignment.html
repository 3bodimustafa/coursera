Poupler Sports

Baseball
Basketball is a team sport in which two teams, most commonly of five players each, opposing one another on a rectangular court, compete with the primary objective of shooting a basketball through the defender's hoop in diameter mounted 10 feet high to a backboard at each end of the court
Boxing
Boxing is a combat sport in which two people, usually wearing protective gloves, throw punches at each other for a predetermined amount of time in a boxing ring.
Football
Football is a family of team sports that involve, to varying degrees, kicking a ball to score a goal.
